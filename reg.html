Documents required for company registeration
  • Application for Registertion
  • Memorandum of association and articles of association of the proposed company
  • Citizenship certificate in case of Nepalese promoter / passport in case of foreign promoter
  • Shareholder's agreement ( if any)
  • Corporate resolution of the shareholder if the shareholder is a company
  • Copy of prior approval of the DOI in case the shareholder is a foreign investor
  • Copy of certificate of registration and other registration documents (memorandum of association, articles of association) if the shareholder is company.
